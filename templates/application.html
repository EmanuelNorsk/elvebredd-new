Application

To apply for a verified badge you have 2 options:

Option 1:

Join our Discord Server and make a ticket. Our staff will be with you shortly and guide you through the process!

Option 2:

Send us an email to ElvebreddContact@gmail.com and say you’re applying for a verified badge. Please do not send an email unless you think you’re applicable! Please provide links to your socials in the email.

Note: We recommend contacting us through our Discord Server as the process will be much faster.
